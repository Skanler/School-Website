about the school

  • P.S. 255 is a multi-cultural school with a diverse population.

    It is situated in the heart of Gravesend, Brooklyn and is part of District 22. Over 37 languages are spoken by our students. We pride ourselves on our ability to teach all students regardless of whether they are new immigrants, gifted and talented, or have special learning needs. Everyone is made to feel at home at P.S. 255 and are respected and valued for their similarities as well as their differences. We work hard everyday to maintain the status that we are highly regarded and recognized for. We consider ourselves to be a school for teaching and learning and we open our doors to other schools, institutions, and businesses to share our best practices that can be replicated.
